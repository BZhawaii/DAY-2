The Orinda House was D-A- Y’s first project and something we are extremely proud of. It is a remodel and addition project located in Orinda, California. From initial planning through construction, we worked very closely with the owners who also acted as the General Contractor on the project. Our challenge was to preserve the original house’s exterior charm despite the needed addition to make the house functional for a growing family. In the end, our clients got a spacious home that respects it’s past, but also feels warm and modern, complete with solar panels.

The Teton House is located in rural Idaho. With unobstructed views of the Teton Mountains, we could not have dreamed up a more beautiful site. Our clients had a need to expand their home to accommodate their large and boisterous family gatherings. We did a complete redesign of the first floor, expanding the kitchen, living, and dining areas. We also added a master suite. This freed up more room in the walkout basement for visitors. Perhaps the best space in the house is the deck that overlooks the valley and mountains. Our clients acted as their own General Contractor and we commend their incredible work.
